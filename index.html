About Us

University of Dayton Student Advocates (UDSA) is an organization dedicated to serving the student community through the education of students on their rights in the conduct system, offering representation and counseling to students during the conduct system process, and working to expand student rights on campus.

We are an organization of students dedicated to freedom of speech and due process. We lobby for change to the University of Dayton conduct system and support students as they move through the conduct system. Our goals include implementing and maintaining a campus that encourages freedom of expression and the marketplace of ideas, as well as a conduct system with high standards of due process.
